Projects

Publication on Principal Component Analysis

Phys. Rev. C 102 (2020), 024911

  • Applied Principal Component Analysis technique to identify the orthogonal modes of correlations in simulation data of heavy ion collision experiments.
  • Studied the physics origin of different modes and checked the impact of dimensionality reduction.
  • Further explored the robustness of the technique by slightly varying different systematic aspects of the correlations and sketched out its stability and limitations.

Publication on Deep Neural Networks

Phys. Rev. Lett. 123 (2019), 052001

  • Trained a deep neural network (DNN) for Observation of rare process of two photons producing two photons in ATLAS experimental data.
  • Used several methods - random sampling, "RELU" activation, cross-validation, 'Adam' optimisation and dropout, to improve accuracy and reduce over-fitting in training.
  • Used the trained model to separate signal events from background events. The deep learning model significantly outperformed the traditional model.

Deep Learning for Particle Identification and Energy Calibration

ATL-PHYS-PUB-2020-018

  • Preliminary ATLAS results on using Deep Learning for particle identification and energy calibration in reconstruction of heavy ion collisions recoreded with the ATLAS detectors.
  • The signal in the calorimeter are converted to pixelated images and fed to classifier and regressor models with different architectures - DNN, CNN and DenseNet.
  • Deep learning approaches outperform the classification applied in the baseline local hadronic calibration methods and are able to improve the energy resolution for a wide range in particle momenta, especially for low energy pions.
  • Used several methods - random sampling, "RELU" activation, cross-validation, 'Adam' optimisation and dropout, to improve accuracy and reduce over-fitting in training.
  • Demonstrated the potential of deep-learning-based low-level hadronic calibrations to significantly improve the quality of particle reconstruction in the ATLAS calorimeter.

Car Accident Severity Prediction

IBM Badge , Github

  • Capstone project, part of the IBM Data Science Professional Certificate.

Bean Disease Classifier

Github
